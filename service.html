Frequently Asked Questions

Are there gluten-free appetizer options? ▾
Yes! Our Mediterranean Salad and Tuna Tartare are naturally gluten-free. We can also modify other dishes upon request. Please inform your server of any dietary restrictions.
Can appetizers be shared? ▾
Absolutely! Many of our appetizers are designed to be shared. We recommend ordering 2-3 different starters for the table to enjoy a variety of flavors.
Do you offer vegan appetizer options? ▾
Yes, our Classic Bruschetta and Crispy Spring Rolls are vegan-friendly. We can also create custom vegan starters upon request — just let your server know!
What's your best-selling appetizer? ▾
The Crispy Calamari and Tuna Tartare are consistently our most ordered appetizers. The Lobster Bisque is also a seasonal favorite during colder months.
