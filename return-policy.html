Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2026

1. Reservation Cancellations

We understand plans change. Here is our reservation cancellation policy:

  • Free cancellation: Cancel at least 2 hours before your reservation time at no charge.
  • Late cancellation: Cancellations within 2 hours of the reservation may result in a cancellation fee of up to 50 JOD for large group bookings (8+ guests).
  • No-shows: If you do not show up without notice, a no-show fee may apply for pre-paid or large party reservations.

To cancel or modify a reservation, call us at +1 (234) 567-8900 or email reservations@cravekitchen.com.

2. Online Order Cancellations

Orders placed through our website or delivery partners can be cancelled under the following conditions:

  • Before preparation: Full refund if cancelled within 5 minutes of ordering.
  • During preparation: Once the kitchen begins preparing your order, cancellation is no longer possible.
  • Third-party deliveries: Orders placed through Talabat, Careem, or Uber Eats are subject to their respective cancellation policies.

3. Refund Policy

We want every guest to leave satisfied. Refunds may be issued in the following cases:

  • Wrong order: If you receive an incorrect item, contact us immediately and we will replace it or issue a full refund.
  • Quality issues: If the food quality does not meet our standards, reach out within 30 minutes of delivery and we will resolve the issue.
  • Missing items: Report missing items within 1 hour of receiving your order for a refund or replacement.

4. Refund Processing

  • Approved refunds are processed within 3–5 business days.
  • Refunds are credited back to the original payment method.
  • Cash payments are refunded in cash at the restaurant or via bank transfer.

5. Catering & Events

  • 7+ days before event: Full refund (minus 10% administrative fee).
  • 3–7 days before event: 50% refund.
  • Less than 3 days: No refund, as ingredients and staff have already been allocated.

6. Gift Cards & Vouchers

Gift cards and promotional vouchers are non-refundable but remain valid for 12 months from the date of purchase. They can be used for dine-in, takeout, and online orders.

7. Contact Us

For refund requests or questions about this policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@cravekitchen.com
  • Phone: +1 (234) 567-8900
  • In person: Visit the front desk at 123 Gourmet Street, Downtown
