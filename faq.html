Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about reservations, our menu, dietary options, and more.

Reservations & Dining

How do I make a reservation?

You can reserve a table through our website using the reservation form, by calling us at +1 (234) 567-8900, or through OpenTable. Walk-ins are also welcome, though reservations are recommended for weekends and holidays.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel for free up to 2 hours before your reservation. Late cancellations for large groups (8+) may incur a fee. See our full Cancellation & Refund Policy for details.

What are your opening hours?

Monday – Friday: 11:00 AM – 11:00 PM
Saturday: 10:00 AM – 12:00 AM
Sunday: 10:00 AM – 11:00 PM
Kitchen closes 30 minutes before closing time.

Is there a dress code?

We have a smart casual dress code. While you don't need to dress formally, we ask that guests avoid beachwear, sleeveless undershirts, and flip-flops.

Menu & Food

Do you cater to dietary restrictions (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free)?

Yes! Our menu includes clearly marked vegetarian (V), vegan (VG), and gluten-free (GF) options. Our chefs can also modify many dishes upon request. Please let your server know about any allergies or dietary needs.

Do you offer kids' meals?

We have a dedicated kids' menu with smaller portions and child-friendly favorites like chicken tenders, mini burgers, and pasta. All kids' meals come with a drink and a small dessert for 6 JOD.

Where do you source your ingredients?

We source fresh produce from local farms and use premium imported ingredients for specialty items. Our meat is locally sourced from certified suppliers, and our seafood is delivered fresh daily.

Delivery & Takeout

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we deliver through Talabat, Careem, and Uber Eats. Average delivery time is 30–45 minutes depending on your location. A minimum order of 8 JOD applies for delivery.

Can I order takeout directly?

Absolutely! Call us at +1 (234) 567-8900 to place a takeout order. Orders are usually ready within 15–25 minutes. You can also order online for pickup.

Events & Private Dining

Can I book the restaurant for a private event?

Yes! We offer private dining for birthdays, corporate events, and celebrations. Our private room seats up to 30 guests, and the full restaurant can be booked for events up to 120 guests. Contact our events team at events@cravekitchen.com for details.

Do you offer catering services?

We provide full catering for events of 20–500 guests. Our catering packages include buffet setups, plated service, and custom menus. Delivery and on-site service staff can be arranged. Contact us at least 7 days in advance.

General

Is there parking available?

Free parking is available in our lot behind the building with 40 spaces. Additional street parking is available along Gourmet Street. Valet parking is offered on Friday and Saturday evenings.

Do you have Wi-Fi?

Yes, complimentary Wi-Fi is available throughout the restaurant. Ask your server for the password or check the card on your table.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, AMEX), Apple Pay, Google Pay, and CraveKitchen gift cards.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to help with anything not covered here.
