Who We Are

Who We Are

CraveKitchen opened in 2010 as a 30-seat bistro downtown. Today we operate a 120-seat restaurant with a private dining room and outdoor terrace. Our kitchen serves Mediterranean, Asian, and American dishes using ingredients sourced from local farms.

We offer dine-in, takeout, and delivery. Our private room fits up to 40 guests for birthdays, corporate events, and family gatherings. We also run a weekend brunch buffet every Saturday and Sunday from 10am to 2pm.
